00006 TurboWavelets.Net provides very fast, flexible and compact implementations of discrete wavelet transformations in C#.
00007 Unlike others this implementation has no limitation is sizes for the transformation (lengths like 39, 739,... are possible, not just power of two numbers) 
00008 At the moment only floating point numbers are supported.
00009 # Features:
00010 - 1D biorthogonal 5/3 wavelet using the lifting scheme (for arbitrary sizes, not just power of 2)
00011 - 2D biorthogonal 5/3 wavelet using the lifting scheme (for arbitrary sizes, not just power of 2)
00012 - 2D haar wavelet (for arbitrary sizes, not just power of 2)
00013 - 2D cascade sorting of coefficients  (for arbitrary sizes, not just power of 2)
00014 - Scale/Crop coefficients in a defined grid
00015 - apply a deadzone
00016 - Multithreaded and threadsafe
